Practical, Research-Backed Solutions

Klimate Consulting LLC (KC) offers a comprehensive suite of research, technical assistance, and consulting services specializing in the industrial, commercial, agricultural, and water sectors. The team provides in-depth technical analysis and tailored solutions on complex technical topics, leveraging strong data analysis skills and years of combined academic and industry experience.

As a technology-forward firm based in San Francisco, KC embraces cutting-edge tools—including AI-powered research synthesis, automated data analysis, and rapid software and web development—to deliver more value within constrained timelines and budgets. This site is our open data lab: a repository of side projects built using AI agents and public datasets that we're making freely available.
